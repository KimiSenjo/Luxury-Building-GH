Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a custom home?

The timeline for building a custom home typically ranges from 8 to 12 months, depending on the complexity of the design, size of the home, and other factors such as weather conditions and material availability.

Can I purchase a home if I live outside Ghana?

Yes, we specialize in helping diaspora clients purchase or build homes in Ghana. Our team handles all aspects of the process, providing regular updates and ensuring a smooth experience regardless of where you are located.

What payment options are available?

We offer flexible payment plans tailored to your needs. For existing properties, we typically require a 30% down payment with the balance payable in installments. For custom builds, payments are structured according to construction milestones.

Do you offer property management services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive property management services for homeowners, especially those living abroad. Our services include maintenance, security, rental management, and regular property inspections.

What areas of Ghana do you build in?

We primarily build in premium areas of Accra including East Legon, Cantonments, Airport Residential, and Trasacco Valley. However, we can also build in other locations based on client preferences and land availability.
